Welcome to the Family Bike Builder Network


Whether seasonally or year-round, for recreation or as a car-replacement, people who put kids on their bikes are faced with a mountain of choices.

The Family Bike Builder Network helps inspire with ideas for components and complete builds from the community, ranging from a budget used bike and seat setup to a fully-loaded electric-assist family cargo bike with all of the accessories.

To get started, take a look at the community builds and community parts people have submitted to see the practical, innovative, and quirky builds people across the world use!

Have your own build? Don't see a part you like? You can add parts to the network yourself. If you want to submit a build, pick some parts on the community parts page and get building!
